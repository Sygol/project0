Tennis tournaments

There are four most prestigious tournaments during a season, that are called Grand Slams:
Date Tournament Location Surface First held
January-February Australian Open Melbourne Hard 1905
May-June French Open Paris Clay 1925
June-July Wimbledob London Grass 1877
August-September Us Open New York Hard 1881
Besided Grand Slams there are also 9 Masters Tournaments:
Date Tournament Location Surface First held
March Indian Wells Masters Indian Wells Hard 1987
March Miami Open Miami Hard 1985
April Mone-Carlo Masters Monte-Carlo Clay 1897
May Madrit Open Madrit Clay 2002
May Italian Open Rome Clay 1930
August Canadian Open Montreal/Toronto Hard 1881
August Cincinnati Masters Cincinnati Hard 1899
October Shanghai Masters Shanghai Hard 2009
October-November Paris Masters Parid Hard 1968
Apart from those tournaments, players compete in smaller ones all around the world.
